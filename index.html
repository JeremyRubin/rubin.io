Hi, I'm Jeremy Rubin.

I'm deeply involved in cryptocurrency, having co-founded Tidbit, the MIT Bitcoin Project, the MIT Digital Currency Initiative, serving on the planning committee for the Scaling Bitcoin conference series since its inception, and serving as an advisor to startups like Lightyear and HomeSidekick. I'm currently focused on improving the core protocols and technology.

Besides cryptocurrency related topics, my other interests include hardware and manufacturing, functional programming, and longboarding.

I graduated from MIT in 2016 with my M.Eng in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science under Professor Ronald L. Rivest and my S.B. in Computer Science and Engineering.

I'm available for talks, workshops, special projects, short-term contract work, or consulting in the cryptocurrency and security space. Just email me.
